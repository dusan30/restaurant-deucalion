ABOUT US

DeucaLion restaurant is located in Spain, in the city of Barcelona, ​​which is full of tourists trough out whole year, especially in summer. This restaurant was opened by the Najdanovic family, who moved from Serbia to Spain in 2000th and in the same year opened one of the most famous restaurants in Spain today. The restaurant is located by the sea, which is very attractive location for tourists. In addition to the beautiful view, the food that restaurant offers is diverse and very tasty. Promising chefs that work in our kitchen are excellent in their jobs and every year they surpass their capabilities to make their specialities even more delicious, and with every meal prepared they make our visitors more astonished by their work.

Deucalion Restaurant offers:

  • Delicious food;
  • Pleasant atmosphere;
  • Beautiful sea view;
  • Music program every weekend from 6 pm.
